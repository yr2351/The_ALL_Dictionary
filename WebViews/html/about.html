About

When people try to look up a definition of an English word, they may use online or paper-based English dictionary.
BUT sometimes, the information from a source is not sufficient to fully understand. Many of them give up understading the word instead searching for another sources again.

You can search the meaning of the term from Merriam Webster's Learner, Merriam Webster, Oxford Dictionary, Urban Dictionary, Wikipedia, Google News, Google Photo and Youtube at once.

How can I search for a word?
  1. Type the word the user wants to look up in the search box.
  2. Click the search icon or press the Enter key.
  3. Wait for a while until the result page appears. (It probably takes 5-7 seconds)
How can I change sources order?
  1. Press the "Change Dictionary Order" button.
  2. Select the order of dictionary source.
  3. The name of the selected source turns red.
  4. If the user selects the same source more than twice, alart message will be appear.
  5. After selecting all sources of order, press the "Change" button.

üìù  Quiz

You can take a quiz based on your notecards!

How can I take the quiz?
  1. Click the "Card" on the navigation bar.
  2. Select the package you want to take a quiz.
  3. Choose the correct answer for each question.
    ‚ú®   If you didn't type definition on your note card, the question would be blank.
  4. Click the "Submit" button, you can see the score.
  5. If you click the "Finished" button, it moves to the quiz page.
How can I see the quiz history?
  1. Click the "Quiz" on the navigation bar.
  2. Click the "History" button.
  3. Select the date of history you want to see.
  4. Click the "Submit" button.
  5. You can check what you got wrong in the quiz.
  6. If you click "Go back" button, it moves to the quiz page.
  7. Or you can check another quiz history by repeating #3 and #4.

üèÖ  Challenge

You can participate in the word challenge for study!

How can I take a word challenge?
  1. Click the "Challenge" on the navigation bar.
  2. Click the "Start" button.
  3. When the questions come out, read the questions and choose the answer.
  4. If you press the "Submit" button or the time limit is up, the answer will be automatically submitted.
  5. You can see your score at the challenge result page.
How can I see the ranking?
  1. Click the "Challenge" on the navigation bar.
  2. Click the "View Rank" button.
  3. You can see the ranking of the word challenge.
  4. If click the "Go back" button, it moves to the challenge page.

For further memorizing, you can make their own notecards and study with them.

How can I organize my notecards?
1. After you signed in with your account, click the Card tab on the top navigation bar to go to noteard page.
2. On the Card page, you may see a button named "Add New Package" on right-top side.
3. Click "Add New Package" button, and give the name for your first card package.
4. Finally, click the "submit" button.

Congratulations! You will see your first card package.
How can I make a notecard?
1. At the notecard page, click "Add New Card" button. Then, you will see making notecard page.
2. You have to choose a card package by clicking upper right side dropdown button.
3. You can add an image from your computer when you click "Choose image" button.
4. After filling in word, part of speech, and definition forms, click "Save" button.

Congratulations! You made your first notecard successfully.
How can I make a notecard with search history?
1. At the making notecard page, you may see search history list on the left side.
2. You have to choose words that you want to make notecards and check the corresponding checkbox.
üëâ   You can add the checked words into the existing package by clicking "Add to Package" button.
üëâ   You can add the checked words into a new package by clicking "Add to New Package" button.

Congratulations! You made your notecards successfully.
How can I see my notecard?
üëâ   From making notecard page, after filling in the all notecard forms and click "Save" button, it automatically shows the notecard that you just made.
üëâ   From notecard page, you have to click the word inside the packaget hat you want to see. Then, it automatically shows the notecard.
How can I edit my notecard?
1. At the showCard page, you can see the edit icon on the top of displaying notecard.
2. Click the edit icon. It will display a form let you edit the notecard.
3. After you finished editting, click the "Submit" button.

Congratulations! You made see the change successfully.
How can I delete my notecard?
1. At the showCard page, you can see the trashcan icon on the top of displaying notecard.
2. Click the trashcan icon. It will delete the notecard right away.

Congratulations! You deleted your notecard successfully.
How can I delete my package?
1. At the notecard page, you can see the "Delete Package" button at the bottom of the list.
2. Click the "Delete Package" button. And check the package that you want to delete.
3. After checked the package, click "Delete" button. Then, it will delete the package right a way.

Be Careful! All the notecards under the chosen package are deleted together automatically.

Make an account, edit user information and delete the account.

How can I change the password?
  1. Click the "My Page" button on the upper right corner.
  2. Click the "edit" text on the right side of password.
  3. Enter the your current password in the "Origin Password" text box.
  4. Enter the new password in the "New Password" text box.
  5. If you enter the right current password, your password will be changed into new password you typed.
How can I change the profile picture?
  1. Click the "My Page" button on the upper right corner.
  2. Click the "edit" text on the right side of the picture.
  3. Select and upload new picture file.
  4. Click the "Submit" button.
How can I change the name?
  1. Click the "My Page" button on the upper right corner.
  2. Click the "edit" text on the right side of the name.
  3. Enter tne new name in the "Name" text box.
  4. Click the "Submit" button.
How can I delete account?
  1. Click the "My Page" button on the upper right corner.
  2. Click the "delete your account" on the bottom of the basic information.
  3. Enter the password in the text box.
  4. Click the "Delete" button, then the alert message will appear.
  5. If click the 'Yes" button, your account will be deleted. (You cannot sign up with the same email)
How can I make an account?
  1. Click the "Sign up" button on the upper right corner.
  2. Enter the email, password, and name.
  3. Click the "Sign up" button on the bottom.
  4. If sign up is successful, the suceess message will appear.
  5. After that, move to the search automatically.
